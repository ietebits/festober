About The Events

Hola folks!!!
Are you that zealous soul who is looking for something exciting in this festive month of "October" ?
Hakuna Matata!!
In this festive month, IETE Students' Forum is ready to quench your thirst for suspense, thrill, and amusement by ensuring that you experience an excitement you've never had, are exposed to an environment you've never been, and participate in an event you've never seen before.
We are thrilled to announce that we are bringing a monumental fiesta "Fest-O-ber". The fiesta consists of three gripping segments.
The first segment, "Clue-E- Minati" compels the cinephiles to cogitate to answer the names of movies and characters based on clues given .
The second segment is for those who enjoy racking their brains to solve riddles and give team work significant value. Are you ready to run, think, and conquer? If yes, then nothing can be more apt than this segment for you.
The third segment will make you go deep into a virtual world. An enormous power of concentration might pave a way for a delicious "supper".
We have a warm-up session to think and answer ready for you ! In the comments section below, answer what the second and third segments are based on and show your competence in winning the fiesta.

Clu-E-Minati

Feed the fanatic in you!

Hey there Cinephiles !! Are you a fan of movies/ web series and are waiting for an opportunity to flaunt your knowledge of dramas and show off your passion ? IETE Students' Forum is all set to usher in a fascinating event Clu-E-minati solely based on the movies and web series.
